An interesting bit of history. The Mathematics Research Center was the target of the Sterling Hall Bombing on August 24th, 1970. The bomb did not go off anywhere near the MRC, and instead killed scientist Robert Fassnacht. The MRC no longer exists, but this sign can be found hidden away in an office in Sterling Hall. Fun scavenger hunt topic.
