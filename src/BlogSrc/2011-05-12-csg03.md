GNU tar for win32 can't actually fork a process for gzip.  What
century is it?  Here's my fix:

    @echo off
    REM untargz.bat
    :AGAIN
    if "%1" == "" goto END
    gzip -d -c < "%1" | tar -xv
    shift
    goto AGAIN
    :END
