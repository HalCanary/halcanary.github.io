telnet to port 80

To check out http headers, you can use telnet, but I've found it easier to use `nc` (netcat):

    H="en.wikipedia.org"
    G="/"
    echo -en "GET $G HTTP/1.1\r\nHost: $H\r\n\r\n" \
        | nc $H 80
