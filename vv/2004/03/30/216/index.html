The past few weeks I've been going to Bluefin Monday nights. Here's why it's cool:

  1. Open pool table.
  2. Decent DJs. The layout of the club allows one to stand around watching the DJs spin. This is educational.
  3. TVs. I get a chance to catch the Daily Show, which I normally miss due to lack of cable.
  4. A playstation at the bar.
  5. Cheap drinks.
  6. No cover.
